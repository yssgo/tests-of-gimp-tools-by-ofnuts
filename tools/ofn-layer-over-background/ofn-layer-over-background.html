ofn-layer-over-background

This is a simple plugin to quickly show one single layer over the image background.

The image background is assumed to be the layer at the bottom of the stack.

To use, either:

  • Right-click the target layer in the layers list, and click the Show layer over background item (at the bottom)
  • Use Edit>Keyboard shortcuts to associate a short cut to python-fu-ofn-layer-over-background. Then hitting the defined key will make the active layer the only one visible over the background.
