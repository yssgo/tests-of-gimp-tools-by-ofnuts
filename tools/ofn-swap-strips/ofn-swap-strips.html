ofn-swap-strips

This plug-in/script cuts two adjacent strips of in the current layer, and exchanges their position. The strip bounds are designated using three guides.

Notes:

  • The layer is assumed to have the same size as the canvas
  • Resulting layers are not merged into the original layer.

The script is started from Layer>Swap strips. There are no parameters.

Example:

Three guides are positioned to indicate the top of the first strip, the boundary between the strips, and the bottom of the second strip.
